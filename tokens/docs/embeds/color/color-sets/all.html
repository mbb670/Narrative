Core Colors

Neutral foundation for the interface itself - text, surfaces, and structural lines.

  • Foreground

    Values for text and icons that sit above backgrounds.

  • Background

    Layered neutrals for pages, cards, and panels, creating depth without adding hue.

  • Border

    Stroke shades that separate content and frame components.

System Colors

Meaningful hues that communicate state or feedback in the product.

  • Critical

    To mark errors, or destructive steps

  • Warning

    Indicating caution or attention.

  • Positive

    Confirm success or healthy status.

  • Informative

    General updates or notices.
